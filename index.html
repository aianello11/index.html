    • What's Trending

    This years current trends are oversized clothing.Oversized silhouettes, baggy jeans, and loose-fitting tops remain popular. Y2K aesthetic Inspired by early 2000s fashion, low-rise jeans, colorful accessories, and baby tees are making a comeback. Denim is always in style,denim in all its forms, from jeans to jackets, is a timeless classic that's always in style. Sheer fabrics and off the shoulder tops have been used to add a touch more feminity and elegance to an outfit, you can use it to dress things up or down.

    Fashion Fails

    -Overly matching outfits: Like matching hat, scarf and bag.

    -Denim on denim: Wearing denim on denim can look outdated.

    -Inapporiate footwear: Wearing the wrong shoes can ruin an outfit.

    -Ignoring body type: Wearing clothes that don't flatter your body shape can be unflattering.
